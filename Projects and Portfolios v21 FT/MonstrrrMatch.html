How it Works

1. Contact

You submit the contact form below. You will receive an automatic email confirming our receipt of your interest which will include information for scheduling your screening.

2. Screening and Background checks

We run a thorough background check and gather any physical, emotional, or mental items that may contraindicate contact with any of our suitors.

Once this process is complete, you will be sent a link to create your profile.

3. Assessment

After gathering details of your likes, dislikes, and other information, we create a list of potential suitors which can be viewed on your profile.

4. Selection

Consultation with you discussing the potential suitors, removing those who hold no potential from your profile and you from theirs.

5. In-House Meeting

If both parties agree, we schedule a supervised meeting between the two (or more) parties to verify mutual interest in a safe environment

6. Exchange of Contact Information

Upon agreement from both parties, contact information is exchanged by our team. The rest is up to you.
